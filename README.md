## Overview

This repository combines two powerful analytical frameworks to drive strategic growth:

1. **RFM Model for Customer Segmentation** - Identify and categorize your most valuable customers using Recency, Frequency, and Monetary value
2. **Cohort Analysis for Marketing Visibility** - Track marketing effectiveness and user retention patterns month-over-month

Together, these tools provide a data-driven foundation for understanding customer behavior, optimizing marketing spend, and scaling your business strategically.

---

## 🎯 What's Inside?

### RFM Model (Recency, Frequency, Monetary)

The RFM model segments customers into meaningful groups based on:
- **Recency**: How recently they made a purchase
- **Frequency**: How often they purchase
- **Monetary**: How much they spend

This segmentation enables targeted strategies for different customer groups, from your best customers to those at risk of churning.

**SQL-based Implementation**: Built with CTEs, window functions (NTH), and quintile analysis for production-ready scalability.

### Cohort Analysis

Track user behavior across time to measure:
- **Merchant Management**: Better understanding and support for top merchants
- **Retention Rates**: Identify cohorts with declining engagement
- **Average TPV Growth**: Monitor transaction value trends across user cohorts
- **Growth Velocity**: Track how your user base evolves month-over-month
- **Marketing ROI**: Measure the real impact of marketing efforts with MOM (Month-over-Month) visibility

This provides marketing teams with clear visibility into campaign effectiveness and helps identify which cohorts are responding to initiatives.
